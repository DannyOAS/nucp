Tips for a Successful Video Consultation

Before Your Appointment

  • Test your camera and microphone at least 15 minutes before your appointment
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Find a quiet, well-lit location for your call
  • Have a list of questions or concerns ready to discuss
  • Have your current medications nearby for reference

During Your Appointment

  • Position your camera at eye level for the most natural interaction
  • Speak clearly and try to minimize background noise
  • Take notes on any instructions or recommendations
  • Ask for clarification if you don't understand something
  • Ensure you know next steps before ending the call
