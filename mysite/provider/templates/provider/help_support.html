Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new appointment slot?

Navigate to the Appointments section and click on "Add Appointment Slot." You can then specify the date, time, and type of appointment.

How do I approve a prescription refill request?

In the Prescriptions section, find the pending request in the "Pending Requests" tab. Review the details and click the "Approve" button if you wish to grant the refill.

How do I use the AI Scribe feature?

During a virtual appointment, click the "Start Recording" button in the AI Scribe section. The system will transcribe the conversation and generate clinical notes that you can review and edit afterward.
