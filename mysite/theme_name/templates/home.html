Patient Portal Features

Appointment Management

Schedule, reschedule, or cancel appointments with ease. Our intuitive calendar interface shows all your upcoming appointments at a glance and sends timely reminders so you never miss an important visit.

  • View your complete appointment history
  • Choose between in-person or virtual appointments
  • Receive automated reminders via email or SMS
  • Submit pre-appointment questionnaires online

Prescription Management

Manage all your medications in one place. Request prescription refills with a few clicks and receive notifications when they're ready for pickup or delivery.

  • View all current and past medications
  • Request prescription renewals online
  • Receive dosage and medication reminders
  • Access educational materials about your medications

Secure Messaging

Communicate directly with your healthcare team through our secure messaging system. Ask questions, discuss symptoms, or follow up on treatment plans without waiting on hold on the phone.

  • PHIPA-compliant encrypted communications
  • Message your entire care team in one place
  • Attach photos or documents when needed
  • Receive notifications for new messages

Health Records Access

Access your complete health records anytime, anywhere. View lab results, immunization records, visit summaries, and more in an easy-to-understand format.

  • Review test results as soon as they're available
  • Track health metrics over time with interactive charts
  • Download and share your records with other providers
  • Receive alerts about important health updates
