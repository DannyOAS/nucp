About Us

At Northern Health Innovations Inc., we are committed to revolutionizing healthcare through technology-driven solutions that enhance efficiency, provider well-being, and patient outcomes.

Our Mission

  • Our mission is to transform healthcare by equipping providers with advanced tools that enhance efficiency and simplify care coordination.
  • Enhance patient outcomes by improving continuity of care.
  • Reduce provider burnout by minimizing administrative burdens and after-hours documentation.
  • Promote the efficient functioning of Canada's publicly funded healthcare system.

Our Vision

To create seamless, technology-enabled healthcare systems that support providers and improve patient care through innovation, automation, and secure interoperability.

Our Values

  • Innovation: Leveraging technology to improve healthcare outcomes.
  • Collaboration: Working alongside healthcare professionals to develop effective solutions.
  • Efficiency: Reducing administrative burdens and enhancing provider workflow.
  • Security: Ensuring data privacy and the highest standards of cybersecurity.

Our Approach

We are excited to have released a provisional model of our care platform in January 2025. The Norhi Unified Care Platform (NUCP)™ delivers automation, seamless care coordination, improved documentation efficiency, interoperable health records, and robust security standards.

As we build on our progress, we're excited to share further updates and anticipate clinical use beginning in Spring 2025.
