Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by navigating to the Appointments section of your dashboard and clicking on "Schedule New Appointment."

How do I request a prescription refill?

To request a prescription refill, go to the Prescriptions section, find the medication you need refilled, and click the "Request Refill" button.

How can I view my lab results?

Lab results are typically available within 48-72 hours after your test. You can view them in the Medical Records section of your dashboard.
